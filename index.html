Vermont

Posted on 12/15/17

Me and my family went skiing in Vermont in the winter. It was very cold. Luckily they had fake snow machines, so it did not have to be below freezing to get good snow. There was also very little ice, so going down the mountains was very smooth.


Florida

Posted on 9/3/23

Me and my family traveled by plane to Orlando, Florida. One of the most memorable things we did was go to a theme park called Universal studios. There was a ginormous roller coaster called the Rip Ride Rockit. The entire coaster is bright red. The part you put your head on has Has speakers where you can pick a genre of music, or the default music.
